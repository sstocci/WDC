About Company

The Intergalactic Space Travel Association is the first succesful intergalactic travel company. We started in February 2021. We have three different planes and we are making many more. We originated in Boston and have expanded to all major cities in the U.S.A.

Our 3 Planes

Adam J. Dunphy V Party Plane: If you are looking for an amazing night in space our party plane is the plane for you. The party plane has everything from great food to fun party games.

Adam J. Dunphy III Tourist Plane: Our tourists plane is great if you are looking to just get out of your house to experience the wonders of space and intergalactic travel.

Mr. Tocci's V.I.P. Experience Plane: The best of the best. It's the Mr. Tocci's V.I.P. Experience Plane with unlimited animal crackers ad water *CD's included*. Play 20 for prizes and learn how to design your own website.
