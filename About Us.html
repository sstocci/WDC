About Us

 

 Our company, The Future, has been working hard for our entire career.  We started as an extremely small group working out of a garage.  My associates Billy, Ethan, and I started with a simple idea that would let us change the world.  We knew that this would entail hours of endless work, but we decided that this was our future.  We worked using different materials like magnets and different elements.  We finally came across our final product and it hit the stores.  It was a hit!  It sold out almost immediately.  We invested all of the money we made back into the company buying warehouses and production facilities, hiring workers and creating new products.  Our company continues to grow and expand to this day.  From a garage to a million dollar company, our hard work truly payed off.
